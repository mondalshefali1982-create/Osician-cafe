Our Story

More Than a
Cup of Coffee

Born from a deep reverence for the ritual of coffee, OBSIDIAN is a sanctuary where every pour is a performance. We source single-origin beans from volcanic highlands and process them with monastic precision — because great coffee demands nothing less.

Our space is designed for the quiet pause — for thinkers, dreamers, and those who know that the best conversations happen over an exceptional espresso.
